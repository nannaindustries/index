About

NANNA Industries is a privately held investment company focused on acquiring, owning, and developing unlisted businesses. We believe in the power of long-term ownership — in building companies patiently, sustainably, and with integrity. Our purpose is to create enduring value by combining industrial expertise with human understanding.

Our Philosophy

We invest in companies with strong fundamentals, capable leadership, and a clear potential to grow over time. Our role as owner is to provide stability, strategic perspective, and capital — while safeguarding the independence and entrepreneurial spirit of each company.

Focus

NANNA Industries focuses on unlisted companies in sectors characterized by structural strength and long-term relevance:

  • Industrial and technical services
  • Healthcare and life sciences
  • Specialized manufacturing
  • Sustainable infrastructure and energy

Our investments are typically majority-owned and developed in close collaboration with management.

Legacy

The name NANNA carries a family legacy — a reminder that all meaningful progress is built on care, patience, and continuity. Our mission is to ensure that this spirit lives on in the companies we build and the people we work with.
